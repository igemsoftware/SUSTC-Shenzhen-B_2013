User's guide:
                      TTEC can predict the efficiency and structure of a rho-independent tanscription terminator in prokaryote.
① In the TTEC page:
1. Please input your terminator sequence in the textbox to be analyzed.
2. You can click the "Example" button to see the terminator sample.
3. You need to click "TTEC RUN" to submit and calculate your sequence.
② In the SBOL page:
1. Please click "Browse" to select your SBOL-file of terminator.
2. You need to click "Upload" to submit the file.
3. You need to click "TTEC RUN" to calculate your sequence.
③ Others
1. The "Experiment Data" link is to the page for our latest experiment data.
2. You can find way to connect us via the link "About us", or visit our wiki.
