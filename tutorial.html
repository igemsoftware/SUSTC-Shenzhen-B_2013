  • how do we evaluate a project?
  • a

1. You can choose to enter Mindroad or CircuitList by clicking the arrow button and clicking the corresponding enter button.

2.Click “Circuits registry”. We arrange projects into nine groups: Art, Energy, Game, Environment Protection, Modeling, Livehood, Industry, Health and Argricuture.

3.Click “Eco900_00” to view the detail information of this project. Eco900_00 is the unique identification number for this project in our database

4.For each project ,we have information on four aspect: general description, protocol, comments, circuits and reference.

5.For each project ,before you add your comment ,you should log in.

6.Click the button at the bottom of the tap, you will enter the Logic Genetic Designer page.

7. LGD is a genetic circuit CAD that can present the detail information of the engineering bacteria and “calculate” the relationship of related parts based on our database.

8. You can get the part information when your mouse over it .

9.Add coding frame and parts freely.

10. You can drag and drop the parts freely, we will generate the relationship dynamically

11. Press Save button you will have a local file xxx.lgd storing your own information about this circuit. And you can open and edit it or shearing it with mindroad

12.Click the “submit” in the homepage, you will enter the submit page. Fill out the table step by step , your project will be uploaded to our server. We will manually check your submission, your project will be added to database.

13.CircuitList is a complete list of all the projects in our database. You can choose the sort order by clicking the sort button . You can also check the latest project by clicking the latest projects button.

14. We have divide the projects into three groups

1 star: reported to be functional in peer-reviewed journals

2 stars: reported by one iGEM team and independently successfully reproduced by another iGEM team

3 stars: reported by one iGEM team"
